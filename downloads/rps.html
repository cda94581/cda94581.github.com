# ChunkBounds - LinusDev

This is a chunk borders resource pack by LinusDev. This shows chunk borders in your Minecraft Bedrock World! This is useful for many farms, as you sometimes need to chunk align them. I also have this constantly on my Blasting to Bedrock world. The link for the pack can be found hereopen in new window.

Note

As of Windows 10 1.16.200+, shaders do not work, including chunk borders. You may be interested in trying out MaddHatter's Chunk Packopen in new window or other similar packs instead.
